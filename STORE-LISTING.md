### 5. Как играть · How to play — `708 / 1000`

The goal is to find all matching pairs in as few moves and as little time as possible.

1. All cards start face-down.
2. Flip two cards on each turn.
3. A match stays face-up; if not, both flip back — remember their spots.
4. Clear every pair to win.

Difficulty: easy — 6 pairs, medium — 10, hard — 12, expert — 14. Higher difficulty means more cards and a bigger reward.
